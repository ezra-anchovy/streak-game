# STREAK Product Roadmap

## Tier Structure

### Free Tier
- All difficulty modes
- 3 hints per day
- Ads during pauses
- Weekly leaderboard

### Basic Premium ($2.99/mo or $14.99 one-time)
- Unlimited hints
- Ad-free
- +50 daily bonus coins

### Advanced Premium ($6.99/mo or $39.99 one-time)
- Exclusive themes/skins
- Multiplier effects on winnings
- Early access to new features
- Training guides

## Implementation Priority

1. **Phase 1: Monetization Foundation**
   - Stripe integration

   - Basic premium purchase flow

2. **Phase 2: Engagement**
   - Theme variety
   - Multiplier mechanics
   - Social challenges

3. **Phase 3: Retention**
   - Leaderboards with competitions
   - Performance analytics
   - Training modules
